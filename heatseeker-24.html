Heatseeker 2024

If you've made it to this webpage, you're probably here to listen to the mixes, so you know the drill.

Heatseeker is a "DJ mix" of sorts that I make when I want to share my taste with the world. I've been feeling like sharing myself with the world less and less these days, but I know that it can sometimes be fun and helpful, so here I am, talking about music I like on the internet.

This year, I made a Best of 2024 mix - almost two hours of my favorite tracks made in 2024 by a relatively wide variety of musicians, if I do say so myself.

But that's not all! I also made some custom mixes for my family for Christmas, and took at least a few hours to put them all together, so I figured I would share those too.

For my parents, I made a mix that tends towards rock - for the appreciators of guitars, real drums, bands, etc.

For my brother Todd, I made a high-energy mix to dance (or work on your feet) to - pop hits, house, electronia, jungle, club music.

For my brother Max, I made a mix that's mostly hip-hop and R&B with some fun outliers and genre-bending tunes.

You can download just the Heatseeker Best of 2024 mix,

or download all Best of 2024 mixes :)
